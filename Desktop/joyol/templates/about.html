✦ Our Mission
We connect people
with great food

Joyol.uz was founded in 2025 with a simple idea: eating out in Tashkent should be easy, enjoyable, and stress-free. We noticed that despite the city having hundreds of incredible restaurants, finding and booking the right one was still frustratingly difficult.

So we built a platform that brings together Tashkent's finest restaurants in one place — with instant reservations, real reviews, and personalised recommendations for every occasion.

Whether you're planning a birthday dinner, a business lunch, a romantic date night, or a wedding reception — Joyol.uz helps you find the perfect table in seconds.
